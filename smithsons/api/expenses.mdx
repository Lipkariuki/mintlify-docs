---
title: Expenses Guide
description: Guide to recording and reconciling trip-related expenses in Smithsons
---

Expenses capture the operational costs associated with executing trips.

## Why Expenses Matter

Accurate expense records help Smithsons teams:

- Measure trip profitability
- Reconcile partner payouts
- Improve pricing decisions
- Audit operational spending

## Common Expense Types

- Fuel
- Tolls
- Loading/offloading fees
- Parking
- Repairs or emergency costs
- Other operational charges

## Typical Workflow

1. Create or submit an expense against a trip
2. Review and verify submitted values
3. Approve or adjust entries (admin workflow)
4. Use totals for reconciliation and reporting

## Example Request

```bash
curl -X GET "https://api.smithsons.co.ke/admin/expenses"
```

<Tip>
Standardize expense categories in your client apps to reduce reporting inconsistencies. Private access credentials are issued separately.
</Tip>
